INTRODUCTION

My name is Vuokko and I was working for the past 6 years as a registered nurse across Finland. During the few last years, I started to examine different kinds of career paths to develop myself as my interest and motivation in the social healthcare sector seem to suspend. Corona and its brought - along burderns towards social healthcare workers seem to make me even more determined to make career changes. In 2021 I quit my permanent job as a team-lead and a registered nurse in elderlycare sector to start full-time study IT.

At first it seemed scary, but the more i study, the more I like what I am studying. I have been taking different online courses and starting to make paths and concrete learning targets. I am far more eager to take part in intensive training course that leads me to real working chances. I find myself a hardworking person who never fears of failing or new things. And life is too short to start doing exactly what bring you joy and interest. As my favourite quote says "Love what you do, and you never have to work a day in your life".
